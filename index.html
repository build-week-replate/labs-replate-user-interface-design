Our mission
Here at Replate, we are geared towards eliminating food waste by bridging the gap between businesses with left over food and non-profit organizations that help feed the hungry. We have a system in place that benefits both the businesses and non-profit organizations. Join forces with us to make the world a better place for everyone.
How It Works
Businesses can request pick ups, after signing up you will request a time and a date that you would like your food picked up, track their driver, and see a flow chart of the impact you are making with your donations (Your donation will reduce food waste and help non-profits to feed the hungry!).
